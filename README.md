# ⚡ EnergyTrack SL

EnergyTrack SL is a Digital Public Good (DPG) web platform designed to help homes and businesses in Sierra Leone monitor electricity usage, reduce energy waste, and manage electricity bills efficiently.

## 📌 Project Overview

EnergyTrack SL addresses the challenge of poor energy monitoring and inefficient electricity consumption in Sierra Leone. Many households and businesses struggle to track their electricity usage, resulting in high utility bills and unnecessary power waste.

This platform provides users with smart tools to monitor, analyze, and optimize energy usage.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Help users monitor electricity usage in real time
- Reduce energy waste
- Improve electricity bill management
- Promote energy efficiency
- Support digital transformation in the energy sector

---

## ✨ Features

- Live electricity monitoring
- Bill estimator
- Usage analytics and reports
- Energy-saving recommendations
- Contact form
- Fully responsive design (Desktop, Tablet, Mobile)
